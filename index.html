This Privacy Policy explains how Andrew Hayes ("I", "me", "my") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the Value Investor mobile application ("the App") available on the Google Play Store. 1. About This App

Value Investor is a free financial data application for Android that displays algorithmically screened stock market data, valuation metrics, and publicly available financial information. The App is supported by advertising through Google AdMob. No subscription fees or in-app purchases are charged to users.

The App is intended for users aged 18 and over. By using the App you confirm you are at least 18 years of age.

2. Information Collected

Information you provide directly:

  • Your agreement to these terms, recorded locally on your device when you tick the consent checkbox on first launch. This record includes a timestamp and the version of the terms you agreed to. It is stored only on your device and is never transmitted.

Information collected automatically by Google AdMob:

The App uses Google AdMob to display banner advertisements. AdMob may automatically collect the following information to serve relevant ads:

  • Advertising identifier (Google Advertising ID)
  • IP address and approximate location (city/region level)
  • Device type, model, and operating system version
  • App usage data and interaction with advertisements
  • Network connection type (Wi-Fi, mobile data)
  • Time zone and language settings

This data is collected and processed by Google LLC under their own privacy policy. I do not have direct access to this data. Google's privacy policy is available at: policies.google.com/privacy

Information I do NOT collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any account information
  • Your financial details, investment holdings, or trading activity
  • Precise GPS location
  • Contacts, camera, microphone, or any device sensors
  • Any data from other apps on your device

3. How Information Is Used

The information collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To display relevant banner advertisements through Google AdMob, which funds the free operation of the App
  • To record your consent to these terms locally on your device
  • To operate the daily automated data update (6:00 AM UTC scheduler) which fetches publicly available financial data from third-party sources on your device
  • To store financial screening results in a local database on your device for display in the App

I do not use your information for any other purpose. I do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with any third party other than Google AdMob as described in Section 2.

4. Data Storage

All financial data and your consent record are stored locally on your device using SQLite — a standard mobile database. This data is:

  • Stored only on your device — never uploaded to any server
  • Automatically deleted when you uninstall the App
  • Not backed up to any cloud service by the App itself (your device's own backup settings may back up app data according to your device manufacturer's policy)
  • Not accessible to me or any third party

5. Third-Party Data Sources

The App fetches publicly available financial data from the following third-party sources. These requests are made from your device directly to these services:

  • Yahoo Finance — market prices, index quotes, and financial ratios for all stocks and indices
  • Financial Modeling Prep (FMP) — valuation ratios for selected US stocks (used as a fallback only when Yahoo Finance data is unavailable)
  • GitHub (AndrewHayes-Dev/ASX300-data) — the ASX 300 stock universe dataset used to populate Australian market screens

These requests may expose your device's IP address to these services as is standard for any internet request. I do not control these services and their own privacy policies apply to any data they collect. The App does not send any personally identifying information to these services.

6. Advertising — Google AdMob

The App displays banner advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob uses your advertising identifier to serve personalised ads. You can opt out of personalised advertising at any time:

On Android: Settings → Google → Ads → "Delete advertising ID" or "Opt out of Ads Personalisation"

If you opt out, you will still see advertisements but they will not be personalised to your interests. The App will continue to function normally either way.

AdMob complies with the IAB Transparency and Consent Framework (TCF) and applicable advertising regulations. For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, and Switzerland, a consent dialog will be shown before personalised ads are displayed, as required by GDPR and the UK GDPR.
